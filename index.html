Do Cyber crime really bother you?

Why do you implement security measures such as locks, security cams, fences, guard and other measures on your private properties? Why do you need registration for your properties? Why everyone is concerned about protecting their valuable assets?

Everyone is concerned about their assets being secure from intruders, trespassers, criminals, burglars and even from neighbors surveillance eyes. Bad guys always try to find loopholes to compromise security of their target. What if your security measures compromised? Oh no, You have a security breach!

Same, What if your information, network, devices, tech life or any digital life got compromised? What if someone breaches on your network? Mm, they can sniff or surveillance your activity, they can steal and use your data, they can take your lifetime savings, they can use your identity and end up you in trouble, they can damage your valuable credibility and reputation, they can bring your life into a tragic end and much more. A personal security breaches can turn your personal life upside down. What if cyber attacks towards the loopholes on government agencies, tech giants, companies, satellite, nuclear weapons and so on? Think about it.

Can you secure your digital data completely? No, cyber criminals always find a way to find vulnerability and exploit them same as in real life. There is nothing as 100% secured on both real and virtual worlds. All we can do is implement and hardening security measures which make the bad guy's life tough and even stay away from you.
